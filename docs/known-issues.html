Known issues

macOS signing and notarization

Current direct-download versions of TidySweep for Mac and TidySnipping are ad-hoc signed and not Apple-notarized. Gatekeeper may require users to approve the first launch in System Settings → Privacy & Security.

Protected files

Some application-related files are protected by macOS or Windows and may require administrator approval. The apps should never bypass operating-system protections.

TidySnipping permissions

Capture will not work until Screen Recording permission is granted. After changing the permission, macOS may require the app to be restarted.

Need help?

Report a bug on GitHub with your operating-system version, app version, steps to reproduce, and a screenshot when appropriate.
