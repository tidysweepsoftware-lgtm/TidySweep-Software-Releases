Privacy policy

TidySweep Software designs its applications to work locally and collect as little information as possible.

Application data

TidySweep and TidySnipping do not require accounts and do not transmit personal files, installed-app lists, screenshots, cleanup results, or usage histories to TidySweep Software.

Internet access

TidySnipping and the normal cleanup functions of TidySweep operate without internet access. TidySweep for Windows may connect to official hardware-vendor resources when the user explicitly starts a driver update check.

System permissions

macOS may request Screen Recording permission for TidySnipping. TidySweep may request access necessary to inspect or remove user-selected application files. Windows may request administrator permission for protected maintenance operations.

External services

Our website, GitHub, YouTube, Buy Me a Coffee, Microsoft Store, Product Hunt, and MacUpdate have their own privacy policies and may process standard web analytics or account information independently.
